Welcome to the Experiment

Thank you for participating in our experiment. The goal is to test your ability to identify specific identifiers in different coding styles. Follow the instructions below:

  1. Fill out the demographics form below.
  2. You will be presented with sentences composed of two or more words, such as "move south".
  3. You'll see a set of identifiers, one of which corresponds to the sentence you saw.
  4. Select the identifier that matches the given sentence.
  5. Complete a series of tasks, some involving camelCase and others kebab-case.
